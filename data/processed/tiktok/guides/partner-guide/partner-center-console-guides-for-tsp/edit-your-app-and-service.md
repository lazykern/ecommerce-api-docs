### Support Republish app/service that has been deactivated
TSP and ISV can republish the APP/Service that has been deactivated by themselves(off-status service/app). You can click "Publish changes" to reactivate this service/app,
Please note:
Only public service that has been approved and deactivated before can be reactivated(eg "under review" service can not be reactivated)
After reactivation, your public service will go through a review process before online
