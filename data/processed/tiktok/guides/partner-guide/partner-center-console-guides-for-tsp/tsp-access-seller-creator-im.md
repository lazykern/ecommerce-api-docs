# **Steps to use Seller-Creator IM**
TSP can utilize the features of Seller-Creator IM if authorized for Full Access or Affiliation. 
After logging into the Seller Center via TSP Access, on the “Affiliate” page, click the “Go to Affiliate Center Home” button to enter the Affiliate Center.

Once in the Affiliate Center, TSPs can use Creator IM just like sellers do through 5 entrances. Please refer to the [Seller - Creator IM User Guide](https://seller-us.tiktok.com/university/essay?course_type=1&from=search%7BcontentIdParams%7D&identity=1&is_new_connect=0&is_new_user=0&knowledge_id=2725932827150123&role=1).

* **Entrance 1:** In the Affiliate Center, click on Creator messages at the top navigation bar.

## FAQ
**Q1: Can I utilize the sub-account in the seller center and TSP access simultaneously?**
In the same Chrome profile, you can either use tsp access or the Seller Center sub-account, which implies that you have to log out if you wish to switch to the other.
