# Considerations before listing your service
## Service discoverability
Discoverability of your service on the **App & Service Store** depends on three key factors:

* **Service type:** Only public services are discoverable on the **App & Service Store**.
* **Seller type:** distinguishing between local-to-local and cross-border sellers.
* **Market availability:** availability of the service is exclusive to sellers operating within that market.

# **Translate and import your service content**
Once you've finished creating your service listing for a specific market, you have the option to utilize the 'Translate and Import from' feature, which allows you to efficiently translate your listings for various markets. However, it's important to keep in mind that the content generated by this feature is based on machine translation and may contain errors or omissions. As the content's accuracy ultimately rests with you, we strongly recommend thoroughly reviewing and verifying the automatically populated information.
