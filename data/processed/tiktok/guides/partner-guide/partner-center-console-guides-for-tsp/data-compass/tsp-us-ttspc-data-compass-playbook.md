# **Data Overview**
## How to use data overview?

* To understand the overall transaction performance and sales rate of the partner's related accounts, and to see the percentage of partner with sales, to determine the activity of its sellers and the partner's ability to sell goods.
* Analyze the live/video content channels to analyze the ability of the partner's bound sellers to sell goods, the number of content releases and the CTR conversion efficiency.

## Detail introduction of functions

1. **Present the sales performance of Partners**

**Core indicatorsÔºö**

* Livestreaming Partners: The number of partners that have livestreamed during the selected period
* Partners with Sales: The number of partners that generated in revenue during the selected period
* Active Rate: (Active Live Partners/Partners) * 100%
* Sales Rate: (Partners with Sales/Partners) * 100%
2. **Present the overall sales trend of Partners**
* GMV (Total): The cumulative amount paid for orders generated from all partner channels during the selected period, including in-house and Affiliate channels, and including returns and refunds
* GMV (Livestreaming): The cumulative amount paid for orders generated from partner livestreams during the selected period, including returns and refunds
* GMV (Video): The cumulative amount paid for orders generated from partner videos during the selected period, including returns and refunds
* Avg. GMV (Livestreams): GMV (Livestreaming) / Livestreams
* GMV (Refunds): The cumulative amount successfully refunded to buyers during the selected period
3. **Show the live sales & conversion trend**
* Livestreams: The number of in-house livestreams/livestreams by partners during the selected period
* Duration: The cumulative duration of in-house livestreams/livestreams by partners during the selected period
* Orders: The number of orders created and paid by users from all partners' in-house livestreams/livestreams
* Product Sales: The cumulative number of products sold from in-house livestreams/livestreams by partners during the selected period
* Avg. CTR: (Number of product clicks / number of livestream views) / livestreams
4. **Show the video sales & conversion trend**
* Videos: The number of videos posted by partners during the selected period
* Orders: The number of SKU orders created and paid by users from partner videos
* Product Sales: The cumulative number of products sold from partner videos during the selected period
* Avg. CTR: (Number of product clicks / number of video views) / number of videos

## Feature value proposition
Video inspirations within TTSPC are intended to provide users with the insights and recommendations required to produce highly engaging and shoppable content. Users can benefit from:

1. Insights and metrics from high-performing videos across categories and date ranges that allows TTSPC users to benchmark their own content against performant and successful shoppable videos.
2. Recommendations and inspiration for scripting and conversation points that can unlock higher engagement and ultimately boost conversion.
3. Visibility on other aspects of high-performance content (e.g. music, hashtags) that will increase are likely to increase impressions and conversion for published shoppable videos.

Ultimately, Video Inspiration for TTSPC aims to empower TTS partners with deriving insights and understanding the drivers behind creating content that will foster engagement and conversion for their managed creators and sellers.

# Q&A

1. Why are the common data indicators in the live room floating?
   * Each data indicator will be compared with the last five minutes and updated every minute
2. how many sellers can get into the live screen at the same time?
   * Max 5
3. What is the query range of the historical live room data?
   * The live room which is after signing the contract can be found, and the live room data during the contract time will be provided.
   * Example: If A signs a contract with the partner on May 1, 2020, A terminates the contract on October 1, and re-signs on July 1, 2021. Then the partner can apply for 2020.5.1-2020.7.1&2021.7.1-current data permissions.
4. Why is it impossible to see video details for some video?
   * Videos, whose status are set to private or deleted, do not support playback and cannot display video pictures;
   * Videos, whose status are set to private and have never been made public, have no transaction or content data;
5. Why are the recommended videos with high revenue not shown in the transaction analysis in the video detail page?
   * For videos with multiple product links, you need to select one product in the video detail page-transaction analysis, and the video of the TOP3 revenue amount in all videos (including the Creator/Merchants' own short video and other Creator/Merchants' short videos) with the same product will be displayed below the transaction analysis.
