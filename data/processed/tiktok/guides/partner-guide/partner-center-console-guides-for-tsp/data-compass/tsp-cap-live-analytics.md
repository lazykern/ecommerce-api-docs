# **Overview**
For **CAP Agencies** managing multiple LIVE simultaneously, it is essential to have a clear and streamlined way to monitor all creators' LIVE performances at a glance. The new **LIVE Analytics** feature with upgraded **LIVE Dashboard** page on TikTok Shop Partner Center, enables CAP Agencies with **an overview of all ongoing and completed livestreams**, helping them quickly identify any livestreams or creators that may need support.
Navigate to: [Partner Center > Analytics > LIVE Analytics](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/compass/live-analysis)
# **What is LIVE Analytics?**
<div style="text-align: center"><img src="https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/1092d591adde4c4195994fedae9402d5~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image" width="2560px" /></div>

**LIVE Analytics** is a one-stop hub where CAP Agencies can:

1. View all sellers and creators' ongoing livestreams and get a high level overview of each LIVE's performances
2. Filter by LIVE status, Creator type, or LIVE starting time to zoom into specific LIVE's that you'd like to focus on
3. Quickly identify LIVEs that are in Violation or Need Attention
4. Access all livestreams and export a custom report
   ... and more! Read on to find out.

# **FAQ**

1. **I'm unable to see any LIVE cards on the Ongoing Livestreams page. Why is that?**
* This is likely because you do not have any bound sellers, or there are no ongoing LIVEs happening at the moment.
2. **I'm unable to see any livestreams on the All Livestreams page. Why is that?**
* This is likely because there were no historical livestreams created by sellers or creators.
