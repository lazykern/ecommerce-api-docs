# TSP Shop Analytics
## Summary
For TSPs that provide Shop Management services, it is important that they can monitor their sellers' shop performances in order to compare top performing shops, review product performances, identify shops that need attention, and more. The **Shop Analytics** page is a one-stop hub that gives TSPs the convenience of accessing all those insights and more via 3 performance modules:

1. Overview
2. Your Sellers
3. Shop Tab

By using Shop Analytics, TSPs no longer need to login to various sellers' accounts via Seller Center to analyze various shop performances separately. Read on to learn how to utilise Shop Analytics, or **visit Shop Analytics by navigating to:** [Partner Center > Analytics > Shop](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/compass/shop-analysis)
*Note: All images shown below are for example purposes only and may not represent the final product.*
## Overview
The Overview tab provides you with comprehensive insights into all your managed shops' performances and is broken down into 4 sections:

* Key Data

## Your Sellers
Here you can access the full list of all your managed sellers and analyze shop-level performance metrics by sorting the table according to the metric you'd like to focus on. For example, sort from highest to lowest GMV, or Items Sold, and so on.

If you have particular shops that you would like to zoom into, simply key in the Shop Name or ID in the search bar. The search bar allows you to search and select up to 3 shops at a time. Upon filtering to 3 shops, it will be reflected on the table below. If you'd like to download a list of all your shop performances, click on the Export button.

## Shop Tab
Through the Shop Tab module, TSPs can now evaluate how Shop Tab contributes to a seller's overall shop performance. Get a breakdown of Shop Tab performance via these 2 sections:

* Key Data
* Sellers you manage

### 3.1 Key Data
Similar to the key data section under Overview, you can view Shop Tab performance metrics here and toggle on the Trend button to easily analyze a comparison of 2 key metrics on the trendchart below.
<div style="text-align: center"><img src="https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/338c5f16171d4dadae4f91c0f22c6f35~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image" width="2383px" /></div>

Click on the pencil icon to customize the metrics shown under Key Data:

* **Shop Tab GMV:** The average amount each buyer paid for orders, including refunds and returns, during the selected time period.
* **Orders:** The total number of SKU orders placed from the Shop Tab during the selected time period.
* **AVG. GMV per buyer:** The average amount each user paid for products after having visited the product details page from the Shop Tab during the selected period.
* **Impressions:** The total number of times your product card was displayed to viewers in the Shop tab. The Shop tab is the entry point from the TikTok app.
* **Product Clicks:** The total number of users who clicked your shop's products from a second-level Shop Tab page during the selected period.
* **Buyers:** The number of unique users who successfully paid for a product after having visited the product details page from the Shop Tab during the selected period.
* **CTR:** The percentage of users who clicked a product after having an impression of it in a second-level Shop Tab page during the selected period.
* **CTOR:** The percentage of users who bought a product after having clicked it in a second-level Shop Tab page during the selected period.
* **Conversion rate:** The percentage of users who bought a product after having an impression of it from this page in the Shop Tab during the selected period.
* **Add-to-cart users:** The number of users who added items to their shopping cart via the current channel.
* **Refunded Orders:** The number of refunded or canceled orders for products purchased after having visited the product details page from the Shop Tab during the selected period.
* **Refunded Amount:** The amount refunded or canceled for products purchased by users who visited the product details page from the Shop Tab during the selected period.
