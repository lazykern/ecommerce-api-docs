# Introduction: What is the TSP Company Profile?
To improve matchmaking efficiency between sellers and TSPs and help merchants to form a comprehensive understanding of TSPs, the TSP profile feature includes the following key functions:

1. Allows TSPs to manage their company profile information on the TikTok Shop Partner Center.
2. Upgrades the TSP Portal (Seller Center and independent website) from a service-level layout to a company-level layout, accommodating scenarios such as sorting, badging, ranking, etc.

TSP can find the company profile information entrance on ++TikTok Shop Partner Center - Profile - company profile information++

## Display TSP Profile on the TSP Portal
After TSP submits TSP profile information, the Seller will be able to check that information on Seller Center-Agencies&Services.
By clicking Learn more, sellers can visit the TSP profile page, where you can check comprehensive information, including:

1. **Partner basic information:** name, logo, company intro, location, contact information, etc
2. **Showcase:** TSPs' previous success stories
3. **Service:** Seller can check all services provided by TSP, and click service to check service details.
4. **Recently viewed companies**
