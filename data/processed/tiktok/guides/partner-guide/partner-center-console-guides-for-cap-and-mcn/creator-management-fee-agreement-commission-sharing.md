**This feature is only available for** [MCN/CAP](https://bytedance.feishu.cn/docx/YVSSdFlS4olNfdxSPjecs8kYnOg) **partners on TTSPC**.
Available Market: UK,ID,MY,VN,TH,PH,SG
## What is commission sharing feature？
In short, a partner that opens a MCN-Creator sharing can obtain a certain percentage of the creator's affiliate commission through the fee agreement with the creator, and after the creator earns the commission, the commission will be automatically credited to the partner's bank card (local MCN)or payoneer account(cross border MCN） according to a certain percentage.
