# **High-level overview**
Ready to be inspired? Video Inspiration for TTSPC (TikTok Shop Partner Center) brings agency partners relevant data and tons of ideas for creating highly engaging content and optimizing their video creation strategy.
Here's what's in store:
üìà Visibility on **critical metrics and data from top-ranked videos, music and hashtags** to help you understand successful content trends.
üí¨ **Identification of drivers** for fostering engagement and transaction, and **recommendations** on how to make your videos more engaging.
üî¨ Drilldown on interaction patterns for top-ranking videos to **understand detailed trends in user engagement.**
Let's dive in!
## **Feature value proposition**
Video Inspiration for TTSPC provides the insights and recommendations needed to produce highly engaging and shoppable content. You can benefit from:

1. Insights and metrics from high-performing videos across categories and date ranges that allow you to benchmark your own content against performant and successful shoppable videos.
2. Recommendations and inspiration for scripting and conversation points that can unlock higher engagement and ultimately boost conversion.
3. Visibility on other aspects of high-performant content (e.g., music, hashtags) that are likely to increase impressions and conversion for published shoppable videos.

Ultimately, Video Inspiration for TTSPC aims to empower TikTok Shop partners with insights and an understanding of the drivers behind the creation of content that fosters engagement and conversion for managed creators and sellers.
## **Access and authorization**
Video Inspiration for TTSPC is available for registered TSP and CAP partners in the U.S. without the need for any specific category permissions.
