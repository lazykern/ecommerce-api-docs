# **Data Overview**
## How to use data overview?
New UpdatesÔºö
Added metrics "Settlement GMV" (30 days) for L2L CAP partners to see how much they can get from TTS based on their managed creators.

* To understand the overall transaction performance and sales rate of the partner's bound creators, and to see the percentage of partners with sales, to determine the activity of its creators and the partner's ability to sell goods.
* Analyze the live/video content channels to analyze the ability of the partner's bound creators to sell goods, the number of content releases, and the CTR conversion efficiency.

## Detail introduction of functions
### Present the sales performance of Partners
**Core indicatorsÔºö**

* Livestreaming Partners: The number of partners that have live-streamed during the selected period
* Partners with Sales: The number of partners that generated in revenue during the selected period
* Active Rate: (Active Live Partners/Partners) * 100%
* Sales Rate: (Partners with Sales/Partners) * 100%

### Present the overall sales trend of Partners

* GMV (Total): The cumulative amount paid for orders generated from all partner channels during the selected period, including in-house and Affiliate channels, and including returns and refunds
* GMV (Livestreaming): The cumulative amount paid for orders generated from partner live streams during the selected period, including returns and refunds
* GMV (Video): The cumulative amount paid for orders generated from partner videos during the selected period, including returns and refunds
* Avg. GMV (Livestreams): GMV (Livestreaming) / Livestreams
* GMV (Refunds): The cumulative amount successfully refunded to buyers during the selected period

### Show the live sales & conversion trend

* Livestreams: The number of in-house live streams by partners during the selected period
* Duration: The cumulative duration of in-house livestreams/livestreams by partners during the selected period
* Orders: The number of orders created and paid by users from all partners' in-house livestreams/livestreams
* Product Sales: The cumulative number of products sold from in-house livestreams by partners during the selected period
* live streamsAvg. CTR: (Number of product live streams of livestream views) / livestreams

### Show the video sales & conversion trend

* Videos: The number of videos posted by partners during the selected period
* Orders: The number of SKU orders created and paid by users from partner videos
* Product Sales: The cumulative number of products sold from partner videos during the selected period
* Avg. CTR: (Number of product clicks / number of video views) / number of videos
