# Overview
For CAP agencies, it is important that they can monitor the performance of their creators and identify top creators and content that is performing well. As a result, we have introduced a new Data Overview page dedicated to presenting the core information needed for operation monitoring, to enhance monitoring and issue finding capabilities for agencies' bound creators, and allow agencies to identify help improve creators posting rate of the products added to showcase.
# **Benefits**

1. **Overview of core business metrics as well as trend comparisons** that allows for further analysis of the core data related to revenue, creator and content performance.
2. **Creator performance diagnostics** that denotes key collaborated creators that need attention and those that have potential to grow.
3. **Easy viewing of top content** (ongoing LIVES and videos) and that helps you to identify creator's performance via trend data and creator growth data.

# **Why use this data overview feature?**
Below we have highlighted use cases for why it's useful for CAP agencies to use this new Data Overview section within the Partner Centre.
## Use Case 1
I want to get an overview and understand trends for my CAP business performance:

* Users will be able to readily view core data metrics for their performance over the selected time frame
* The list of metrics available at this time will include:
   1. Affiliate GMV
   2. Est. Commissions
   3. Commission base
   4. Items sold
   5. Affiliate Orders
   6. Direct GMV
   7. Direct refund GMV
   8. Refunded items
* Additionally, users can choose to view and compare trends across these core data metrics for the selected time period

# Feature walkthrough
## Date filter

1. You can easily select the date range for the data on the top left side. This date range will apply to all succeeding metrics displayed
2. By default the last 7 days will be selected.

## Core Data

1. The core data section will display the following aggregated metrics:
   1. **Affiliate GMV:** The total amount of paid orders (including returns and refunds) attributed from this affiliate within 14 days of clicking product links in their content.
   2. **Est. Commissions:** The amount of commission we estimate creator can earn on all paid or unpaid cash-on-delivery (COD) orders, non-COD orders, and refunded orders.
   3. **Commission base:** The amount of order refunds generated from this creator the selected period.
   4. **Items sold:** The number of products sold through this affiliate during the selected period, including items in refunded orders.
   5. **Affiliate Orders:** The total amount of paid orders generated from Affiliates
   6. **Direct GMV:** Direct GMV is the total amount paid for orders including returns and refunds. This excludes any form of attributed GMV
   7. **Direct refund GMV:** The cumulative amount refunded to buyers during the selected period based on Direct GMV achieved. This means that the Refund GMV only calculates GMV from refunded orders based on buyer's last clicked content type and excludes any form of refunded orders from attributed GMV.
   8. **Refunded items:** Number of items with refund from affiliate orders during selected time period

<div style="text-align: center"><img src="https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/f76cd4a74af640f586bd7618f63bbfc3~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image" width="2067px" /></div>


2. Below each metric you can find the % deviation (increase or decrease) of the metric over the selected date range:
   1. Green color reflecting positive changes (increase in % vs previous period)
   2. Red color reflecting negative changes (decrease in % vs previous period)
3. You will find the toggle to compare **Trends** and when switched on, this will allow you to compare up to 2 core metrics with each other. The trends of these two selected metrics will be displayed over the selected date range in a graph.

### Core Metrics

* **Creators with sales:** The number of creators that generated in revenue during the selected period
* **Creators posting contents:** The number of creators posting videos or having livestreams during the selected period
* **Creators with adding products:** The number of creators adding products into the showcase during the selected period
* **Authorized Creators:** The number of creators that agreed authorization during the selected period
* **% of creators with sales:** This is calculated as **** *Creators with sales / Authroized Creators * 100%*
* **% of creator posting content:** This is calculated as *Creators posting contents / Authroized Creators * 100%*
* **% of creator adding products:** This is calculated as **** *Creators with adding products / Authroized Creators * 100%*

Similarly to the **Core Data** section above, you will find the toggle to compare **Trends** and when switched on, this will allow you to compare up to 2 core metrics with each other. The trends of these two selected metrics will be displayed over the selected date range in a graph.

### Creator ranking
This displays up to the top 20 creators that have been ranked by **GMV** or by **Order** that have generated.

* In addition to the Creator, further information will be available regarding:
   * Creator name
   * Contribute %: this is the amount of GMV the creator has generated for the agency
   * GMV: this is the GMV generated by the Creator
      * % deviation in GMV generated (compared to previous period)
* You can also select **[View All]** and this re-directs to the Custom Report page where you can find information for all the creators you have a binding relationship with.
* You can also click on the creator name which will direct you to the Creator Analytics page.
