## **What is Find Creators？**
Find creators helps CAPs discover and partner with TikTok’s Creator community.
## **How It Works？**

* Find creators is a set of features that enable CAPs to connect and collaborate with Creators.
* CAPs can view the contact information of the creator or send an invitation with one click.
* CAPs can search for creators by using filters such as Creators, Followers, and Performance.

## **What type of creator can be found on Find Creators？**
Creators who have e-commerce permissions and are not official or marketing accounts.
## **How to use Find creators？**
### **Step 1 - Navigate to Find creators**
Log in to your TikTok Shop Partner Center account and click "**Creator Management**" followed by "**Find creators**".

### **Step 4 - Invite the creator**
After finding a creator you would like to work with, click the invite button.
<div style="text-align: center"><img src="https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/53eb2d746c634d8fb86ad01cafb0dffc~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image" width="2120px" /></div>

You will need to select the invitation purpose, provide a message to the creator (no more than 500 words), and your contact information (at least one type contact) in order to send an invitation.
**Invitation purpose**: For CAPs, please choose "Establishing a long-term partnership with the creator". For affiliate partners, please choose "Inviting the creator for product distribution".
**Message to creator**: We recommend sharing a brief overview of your agency along with the advantages of working with you.
**Contact information**: We recommend providing your WhatsApp, email address, and phone number to provide creators with multiple options to reach out to you.

## **FAQ**
**Q1: Why is the contact information of some creators unavailable?**
A creator is able to turn off displaying information causing their contact information to not be displayed on Find Creators.
**Q2: Is there a limit to how many invites I can send out?**
A CAP can only send out 100 invites a day to creators.
