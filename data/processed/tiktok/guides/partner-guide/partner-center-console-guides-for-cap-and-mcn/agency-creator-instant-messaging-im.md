**Note:** *Agency-Creator IM is available to* ***Creator Agency Partners (CAP)*** *and/or* ***TikTok Shop Affiliate Partners (TAP)*** *- manage and work directly with creators.*
# Overview
Connect directly with creators through TikTok Shop's Agency-Creator IM system. Send instant messages within the platform - no external messaging needed. Track creator engagement seamlessly.

* **Build Direct Relationships** - Send invites, negotiate, and collaborate in one place for faster results
* **Streamline Your Outreach** - Share products with verified creators and track real-time engagement
* **Grow Your Network** - Efficiently manage multiple markets and creators in one professional space

# Messaging Features & Guidelines
## Message Limits
Send up to 5 messages per creator monthly before receiving a response. Enjoy unlimited messaging with linked creators and active collaborations.
## Who Gets Access
The feature is available for both CAP and TAP Agencies. The IM function entry point will be displayed on the TTSPC navigation bar when you log in select CAP, TAP or All Roles.
<div style="text-align: center"><img src="https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/8c5c5767d7e24c71a070a5814db2921c~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image" width="2298px" /></div>

Sub-accounts do not automatically have access to the IM feature. To grant access, go to **My Account** > **Team Members** > **Add New Role**, and check "**Instant Messaging with Creators"** for sub-accounts.
## Start a Conversation
Access instant messaging through five convenient entry points in TikTok Shop Partner Center:

1. TTSPC navigation bar
2. Find Creators page
3. Manage Partnerships
4. Partner Campaigns details
5. Partner Campaigns product sharing
