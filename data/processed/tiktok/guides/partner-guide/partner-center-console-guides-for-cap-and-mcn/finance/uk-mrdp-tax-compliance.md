## **What is the UK MRDP Compliance?**
**UK MRDP,** which is short for **UK Model Reporting Rules for Digital Platforms,** is a tax regulation requiring partners on digital platforms to report specific tax information. Complying with the UK MRDP helps you avoid penalties, payout blocks, and potential account deactivation. But not to worry, we are committed to providing you with all the essential tools and information to navigate the process effortlessly.

---


## **Do You Need to Report?**
If you have received any commission as a TikTok Shop e-commerce partner, you are required to comply with the UK MRDP.
**Tip:** Make sure you understand the reporting requirements and deadlines. Staying informed will help you navigate compliance smoothly and avoid any issues with your account.

## **What Information is Required?**
Here's a summary of the different requirements for **UK TTS Partners:**
| **Requirement** | **Partners** |
| --- | --- |
| **Basic Information** | - Primary address <br> - Legal name |
| **Tax Identification Number (TIN)** | - TIN number required (company registration number) |
| **VAT ID Number** | - VAT Registration Number if VAT-registered <br> - Option to select "No" if not registered |
| **Additional Details** | - Company registration number required |

**Tip:** **Prepare your VAT and TIN documents** to ensure quick and easy submission.

## **Yearly Confirmation Requirement**

* **Annual Confirmation:** Starting next year, partners must confirm the accuracy of their UK MRDP information each year by December 31.
* **Penalty for Non-Confirmation:** Payouts will be blocked if confirmation isn’t completed for three consecutive years.

**Action Required:** Review and confirm your UK MRDP information annually to maintain uninterrupted payouts.

## **Summary Checklist for UK Partners:**

1. **Verify Eligibility:** If you received any commission, you need to comply.
2. **Prepare Documentation:** Ensure TIN and VAT details are ready for submission.
3. **Respond to Notifications:** Act on reminders to avoid enforcement actions.
4. **Confirm Yearly:** Starting next year, Review your UK MRDP details by December 31 each year.
5. **Keep Information Updated:** Stay on top of submission and verification deadlines to ensure continued access to payouts.
