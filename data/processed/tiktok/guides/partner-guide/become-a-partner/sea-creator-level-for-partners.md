# What is Creator Level?
**Creator Level** is a season-based program that gives Creators a set of tasks in order to level up and receive rewards.

* Creators are ranked from Level 0 (L0) to Level 8 (L8), with levels refreshed at the start of each month based on the previous month’s performance. Performance is evaluated using GMV, activity level, and product selection and e-commerce capabilities.
* **Higher Creator Levels** reflect stronger GMV generation and consistent affiliate activity. They also unlock greater benefits and support from the platform.
