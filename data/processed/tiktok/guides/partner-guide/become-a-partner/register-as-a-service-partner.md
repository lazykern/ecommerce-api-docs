# Getting Started on TikTok Shop Partner Center
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/ab3349b3334b492bb6867a5a4a5476a0~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image)
## Step 1: Create an account
Create an account with your email and password. Phone number is optional.
ðŸ“Œ Please keep in mind that your email address will serve as the primary channel for receiving important platform notifications, so make sure it's valid and up-to-date.
**Important:** Be sure to use the Partner Center site specific to your market and company's business certification:

* If you are servicing creators or sellers in the **US market,** create an account at [partner.us.tiktokshop.com/account/sign-up](https://partner.us.tiktokshop.com/account/sign-up)
* If you are servicing creators or sellers in **non-US markets**, create an account at [partner.tiktokshop.com/account/sign-up](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/account/sign-up)

**Note:** The TikTok Shop Partner Platform and Open Platform merged into TikTok Shop Partner Center in late 2022. If you possess a TikTok Shop Partner Platform or Open Platform account, you can log in using your existing credentials on Partner Center.
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/5b614118c0db4a668854b0bd5d5af528~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image)
## Step 2: Select your category and market
After creating an account, you'll find a business guide tailored to different partner types. Pick your partner type from this guide, click on 'Start Business,' and it will lead you to the 'Get Started' page to initiate your application for your selected category.
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/3a616f116d7f40e9bd68d367d9e10310~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image)

* **Registration Region:** Please ensure the region is consistent with your company's business certification.
* **Target Market**ï¼š
   * Cross-border partners (registration region is China and/or Hong Kong, China) have the option to select (US, UK, MY, VN, TH, PH, SG, ES, DE, FR, IT, JP) as their target market.
   * Local partners (entities in the local market) should choose only the local market as their target market.
* **Business Category:** Select your business category.

ðŸ“Œ The available business categories may vary depending on the registration region and target market. Learn more about [business categories for different markets](64f1989064ed2e0295f3c1b5).
### Select your category and market in Bulk
You can choose categories(Maximum of 4 categories) **in bulk** and fill in the required info on one page.
Note: if you are also applying for the Developer category, you can click "get app key now" to gain the app key and start building your app.

## Step 3: Fill in the required information
You'll need to complete the necessary fields according to the category you've selected.
Tips: You can refer to the [partner application requirements](64f19899cb677b0286e75d67) for more information and have your documents prepared in advance.

# Managing your account
To manage your account information, such as login email, phone number, and password, go to **My Account>>Profile** page.
If you wish to change your login email, phone number, or password, simply click on the respective email address, phone number, or password on the right-hand side. You'll be prompted to verify your identity through an email or phone verification code before you can successfully update your email address, phone number, or password.
