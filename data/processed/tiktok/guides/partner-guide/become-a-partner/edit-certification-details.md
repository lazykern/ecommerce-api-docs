### **Why do we support partners editing the certification detailsÔºü**
Currently, the partner is required to provide certification details to join TTSPC. Once the platform has reviewed and approved certification details, these certification details cannot be changed. However, there may be instances where a partner needs to update their certification details, such as their name, logo, etc.

### Step 3 Edit certification details and submit
You can edit the files according to your needs and click "Submit" to trigger the review process.
**Please note:**

1. There are **certain sensitive files** related to the entity that cannot be changed, including the company's legal name, registration date, business documents, business owner information, etc.
2. Editing and resubmitting these files **will not affect the existing certification information** unless it has been approved.

## FAQ
**Q1: Why can't I modify some informationÔºü**
Entity information is sensitive and unique information at the company level that cannot be changed, including the company's legal name, registration date, business documents, business owner information, etc.
