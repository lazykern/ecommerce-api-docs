# Step 1: Register an account
You can register an account with Email, Phone number (optional), and password.
**Please note that* the *email address is* critical *and will be used to receive platform emails. Please make sure this is a real and valid email address.*

# Step 2: Select Partner Role and provide entity information
The partner needs to choose a **partner role.** As a US local agency, your registration market will be set as **US** by default, and you need to provide **entity information** to verify your company.
Note: Please ensure that the company information provided is accurate. Once approved, the partner's entity information will be applied across all categories and finance-related information, and can not be modified.

# *Experience For Existing Agency Partners
### New Service Category for All Agencies
The service category upgrade will take effect from April 30. All existing partners might notice a slight difference in your system.
**⚠️ Please note:** 

* This upgrade would not affect any related feature usage according to the different partner types
* From **April 30th,** new partners will be **unable to register** for the discontinued categories of ++Mass Recruiting, Mass Tutoring, and Paid Media++**.**
* From **June 5th**, **obtaining new authorization** from sellers for the discontinued categories of ++Mass Recruiting, Mass Tutoring, and Paid Media++ will no longer be possible.


![Image](https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/c04e9c4573ae41f99de6b17ec5865045~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image)

Together with the service category update, for the role switch button on other product pages, like payments and analytics pages... The role will reference the latest service category structure to retrieve the corresponding data.

### Check the existing onboarding status and information 
Your application status for each category will be **kept the same** as before. Your onboarding information will be integrated into a new user interface framework.
You can view your entity information under the "Company Entity Info" section and access related category information and review statuses under the "Service Category Info" section.
**Please note:** During the TTSPC upgrade, your onboarding application will be rejected, while the system will automatically generate another application under the same category. After the upgrade, your application will be processed. We appreciate your patience and kind understanding and apologize for any inconvenience caused.
