# What is Creator Agency Partner (CAP)?
Creator Agency Partner (CAP) incubates and supports creators within the TikTok Shop ecosystem. These agencies either manage, represent, or have access to creator talents in the US and serve TikTok Shop by acquiring, educating, and incubating creators to produce Short Video + LIVE e-co content at scale.
