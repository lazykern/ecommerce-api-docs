# Key Mechanisms 
Partner Campaigns Ads Commission Dual Attribution is a new update for TikTok Shop Partner (TSP) . This enables sellers to set a campaign total **shop ads commission rate** for products in partner campaigns, while automatically distributing commissions between partners and creators based on attribution results. 
Here's the key mechanisms in Affiliate partner Shop Ads.

* Currently, the affiliate partner Shop Ads function is available in the US region.
* Sellers can set the rates in new or ongoing campaigns from Agency campaigns, Seller campaigns and Tiktok Shop Exclusive campaigns. The rate cannot be changed after setup.
* Only when sellers set the Shop Ads commission rate for products in campaigns, can partners see this rate in Campaigns and Product pools in Partner center.
* If sellers set the Shop Ads commission rate for products, when a partner sets the affiliate commission rate, the ads commission distribution ratio between the partner and the creator is the same as that of the affiliate by default. The system calculates and automatically displays the ratio accurately to two decimal places. 
* If the seller does not set the TAP campaign ads commission rate and runs ads, the advertising orders will be settled according to the TAP campaign affiliate commission rate.
* Attribution logic for affiliate and ads commission: Essentially the same as open collaboration. In summary, if the affiliate and ads attribution are to the same creator, the settlement will be based on the ads commission rate; in other cases, it will be settled based on the affiliate commission rate.

| 3. Seller has Shop Ads Commission Rate set for a Creator's video and Buyer adds the product from the **Creator's video found in the Creator's account.** |  |  | **10% - Standard** |
| 4. Seller boosts the Creator's video with no Shop Ads Commission Rate set. |  |  | **10% - Standard** |
