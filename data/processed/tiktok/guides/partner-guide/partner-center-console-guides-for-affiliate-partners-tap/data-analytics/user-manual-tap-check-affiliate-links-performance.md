# Summary
Creators can now share affiliate links on external platforms to boost commissions. As such, it is important for TAP agencies to monitor their creators' affiliate link performances to optimise this new channel of commission generation. With that, we have introduced a new module called **Links** that allows TAP agencies to quickly grasp creators' overall affiliate links performances.
To get a quick refresher of how affiliate links work for Creators, feel free to read the flowchart below:

# Benefits of Monitoring Affiliate Link Performance

1. **Identify Creators' Link channels with high traffic** to guide Creators in optimising the relevant traffic channels to boost commission.
2. **Easily view overall Affiliate Links attribution towards key metrics like GMV, Orders, Est. Commission and more** to understand the effectiveness of affiliate links for Creators.
3. **Engage with Creators with a custom performance report** where you can analyse how Affiliate Links impact different dimensions and form a different affiliate link strategy for each Creator.

# How to Export A Custom Report
Navigate to: [Partner Centre > Analytics > Custom report](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/compass/custom-report)
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/dd2ffd3617d241c09d61984d24ebaf53~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image)

1. **Dimension:** Select any of the dimensions below to automatically include affiliate links metrics in the custom report shown. Click Export.
   * Campaign
   * Creator
   * Product
   * Shop
   * Product Category

*Affiliate Link metrics will not be displayed upon selecting the LIVE or Video dimension.*
For more information on Affiliate Link metrics shown on a custom report, refer to the definitions below.
| **Metrics** | **Definition** |
| --- | --- |
| Link GMV | The total amount paid for orders from affiliate links shared by creators, including returns and refunds. |
| Link Items sold | The total number of items sold from affiliate links shared by creators, including returns and refunds. |
| Link Orders | The total number of paid orders placed from product links creators shared. |
| Link Creator Estimated Commission | The total estimated commission on orders from affiliate links shared by creators. This includes commissions for paid and pending COD orders, non-COD orders, returns and refunds. |
| Link Partner Estimated Commission | The total estimated commission paid to partners on orders from affiliate links shared by creators. This includes commissions for paid and pending COD orders, non-COD orders, returns and refunds. |
