# Overview
TikTok profile linking for TikTok Shop agency partners is a new feature that connects your TikTok business profile to your agency account. It helps build your credibility and makes recruiting creators easier. By linking your profile, you can showcase your work directly on TikTok, helping creators trust your agency more.
Here are the key benefits:

1. Build Credibility: Show your work and partnerships on TikTok, increasing trust with creators.
2. Easier Recruitment: Stand out to top creators and simplify the process of connecting with them.
3. Better Insights: Use TikTok's marketing tools to understand what works and improve your creator strategies.

## Step 2: Generate QR code to link with Agency's TT Account
If you do not have a TT account, you can go through the sign up flow. If you do have a TT account, proceed to "Get started"
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/d67a123a03fd44b1bd5f9f4ce33a2f94~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image)
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/e563f76d894e49038b7311a306fdb399~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image)
The QR code will automatically generated. There are linking requirements for the TT official account :

* Account has to be 18 years old
* Active with no violation history
* Can't be a registered business account, you will have to switch back to a normal TT account in order to perform the linkage task
* Can't linked with another agency or creator network
