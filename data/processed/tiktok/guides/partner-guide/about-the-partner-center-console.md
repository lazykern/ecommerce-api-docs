Welcome to the TikTok Shop Partner Center Console end user documentation!
The [Partner Center Console](https://partner-sso.tiktok.com/account/login) is the web application used by TikTok Shop Partners of all types to create and manage the services they offer to sellers and creators, and streamline daily operations. The TikTok Shop Partner Center Console simplifies collaboration processes and offers valuable insights into your business.
If you're just getting started and haven't yet determined which type of TikTok Service Partner you are or you haven't yet onboarded, please start by learning more about [TikTok Shop Service Partner types](6789f6fd2dccb8030e8de98b).
