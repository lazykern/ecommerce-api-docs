We get it - things change.

Whether there's updated UIs to show, new branding to match, or new features to call out, this guide will show you how to edit your app listing to ensure the team is happy with what TikTok Shop merchants see when they navigate to our App Store.
# How to edit your App Listing
To edit an existing App Listing, follow these steps:

1. From the main TTSPC Page, click **App & Service** on the left-hand navigation bar.
   ![Image](https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/eden-sg/jvK_ylwvslclK_JWZ%5B%5B/ljhwZthlaukjlkulzlp/Developer_Guide/Develop%20and%20launch%20a%20TikTok%20Shop%20App/Edit%20your%20App/Zight%20Recording%202024-11-01%20at%2001.41.11%20PM.gif)
2. Click on the relevant App you wish to edit, and use the ellipses (i.e. three dots) to open more options. Select **Listing Review**.
3. Select **Edit Draft** to start editing or updating your App Listing
   * For detailed information on what each Field or area means and how it translates to the App Store, please see the following section.

![Image](https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/eden-sg/jvK_ylwvslclK_JWZ%5B%5B/ljhwZthlaukjlkulzlp/Developer_Guide/Develop%20and%20launch%20a%20TikTok%20Shop%20App/Edit%20your%20App/image%20(12).png)

4. As you edit your App Listing, please be sure to hit **Save** at the bottom of the screen to save your progress. When all edits have been made, hit **Publish Changes** in the top-right section.
   * Please note -- after selecting **Publish Changes**, your App Listing changes will go into a Review state.
   * From here, the TikTok Shop App Store Team will receive an approval request to review and approve the changes.
   * Once the listing request is approved, all changes will be immediately live in the App Store.

![Image](https://sf16-sg.tiktokcdn.com/obj/eden-sg/jvK_ylwvslclK_JWZ%5B%5B/ljhwZthlaukjlkulzlp/Developer_Guide/Develop%20and%20launch%20a%20TikTok%20Shop%20App/Edit%20your%20App/image%20(13).png)
