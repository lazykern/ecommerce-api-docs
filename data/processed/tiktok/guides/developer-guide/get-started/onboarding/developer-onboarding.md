To register as a developer partner, follow these steps:
### Before you begin: determine your developer type

When you register as a TikTok Shop developer, you'll be prompted to select one of two types of developer:

1. TikTok Shop seller developer; or
2. App developer or system integrator (ISV).


The primary distinction between these two developer types is app developers and system integrators (ISVs) can build **public** apps and request authorization to access the data for sellers on TikTok Shop, while seller developers can build **custom** apps that can access their **own** seller data only. That is, seller developers can't request authorization to access any other TikTok Shop seller's data.

Note that seller developers are required to have an activated TikTok Shop account **before** they can register as a developer on Partner Center. Also, the email you've used for verification must be the same email used to register the TikTok Shop. There's no option to provide an alternative email.

## Final step: initiate the compliance and legal review process
Once you've successfully registered as a developer partner, you must initiate the compliance and legal review process, which can take three or more weeks to complete. Compliance and legal reviews are required on a case-by-case basis for some partners, but they are always required for US and UK partners. Contact the Partner Development & Management team for your market and request a due diligence questionnaire for your region.

* US: [partner.us@tiktokshop.com](mailto:partner.us@tiktokshop.com)
* UK: [partner.uk@tiktokshop.com](mailto:partner.uk@tiktokshop.com)
* Other: [partner@tiktokshop.com](mailto:partner@tiktokshop.com)

Refer to [Compliance and Legal Review](data-security-and-privacy-review) for more information on the process.
