## Incident Statistics
![Image](https://p16-arcosite-va.ibyteimg.com/tos-maliva-i-10qhjjqwgv-us/4c8b3ccaee914d95bc1824c41cbae9f6~tplv-10qhjjqwgv-image.image)
TikTok Shop Incident Statistics are generated by integrating with TikTok's internal Quality Engine product through Quality Engine APIs.
<span style="background-color: rgb(255, 245, 235)">ðŸ‘‰ </span><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 245, 235)"><strong>Note</strong></span><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 245, 235)">: Quality Engine is an internal order reconciliation system that compares </span>[orders](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/order-api-overview)<span style="background-color: rgb(255, 245, 235)"> and </span>[fulfillment](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/fulfillment-api-overview)<span style="background-color: rgb(255, 245, 235)"> statuses for TikTok Shop orders between TikTok Shop Seller Center and the external platform where the orders are synched and fulfilled by the seller. Quality Engine's order and fulfillment data reconciliation system proactively alerts sellers to orders that are already cancelled by the system for failing to meet </span>[`shipping_due_time`](https://partner.tiktokshop.com/docv2/page/sla-information-now-available-in-orders-api)<span style="background-color: rgb(255, 245, 235)"> SLA due to issues in order synching or fulfillment from the external platform, or are at the risk of being cancelled by the system for failing to meet `shipping_due_time` SLA.</span>
In order to use Quality Engine Incident Statistics in Partner Center Developer Dashboard, and for maintaining the required Partner close rate for Incidents, it is recommended for all developers to integrate their TikTok Shop Developer Apps with Quality Engine APIs available here: [API Documentation - Quality Engine Order Data Exchange API](https://bytedance.sg.larkoffice.com/docx/A6qMdwPC5oESvkxLuffcAVMbnLc)
There are two different types of Incidents for TikTok Shop Orders that developers and partners are alerted to, and must address and close using the Incident Feedback and Close Process in document below.
### A- LOC Incident Statistics
<span style="background-color: rgb(255, 245, 235)"><strong>A-LOC stands for "App Caused-Late Dispatch Rate TikTok Shipping Cancel Order Count"</strong></span><span style="background-color: rgb(255, 245, 235)">:</span>
TikTok Shop Orders that are cancelled by the system for missing order `shipping_due_time` SLA, and are cancelled by the reason `system_cancel_order_reason_shipping_timeout` are counted in the system as A-LOC. It is important to prevent A-LOC orders as much as possible to avoid loss of order revenue for both sellers and TikTok Shop. TikTok Shop Partners are recommended to keep **A-LOC rate < 0.01%.**
