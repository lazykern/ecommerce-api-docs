## What is changing?
The previous deadline of hazmat requirements have been extended from 2025-01-05 to **2025-03-05**. Developers are encouraged to make changes prior to this date.
## Which markets and versions are affected?
All US and UK local sellers are impacted by this change. The change is applicable to all API versions.
