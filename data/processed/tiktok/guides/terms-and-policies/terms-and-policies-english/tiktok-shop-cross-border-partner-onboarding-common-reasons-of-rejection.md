This document is intended solely for TikTok Shop's cross-border partners as a self-assessment guide to identify and address common issues that may lead to the rejection of onboarding applications.

# Business License and ID
**1.1 Insufficient Information**
> Including but not limited to:

> * Partial or incomplete images (e.g., not the entire document is shown);
> * Documents with creased corners, causing information to be obscured;
> * Blurry or low-resolution images;
> * Missing back side of the legal representativeâ€™s ID card;
> *  Non-original or altered documents.


**1.2 Expired Qualification**
Including but not limited to:
An expired ID card or one that is nearing expiration;
An expired business license or one that is nearing expiration.
*Note: Qualifications are considered "nearing expiration" if their validity is less than 30 days.*

**1.3 Qualifications Held for Less Than One Year**
The submission date in the partner system is less than one year from the establishment date on the business license.

**1.4 Unrelated Business Qualifications**
Including but not limited to:
The company's business qualifications are not related to the applied category (e-commerce operations).

**1.5 Inconsistent Entity Information**
Including but not limited to:
A mismatch between the company name on the business license and the name provided in the application (please include the original Chinese name and provide a Chinese translation if necessary);
Discrepancies in the legal representative's information compared to the business license.

**1.6 Repeated Onboarding**
Including but not limited to:
Using the same business license, mobile phone number, or email address across multiple applications;
Overlapping information with high-risk associated partners who have previously been, or are currently pending, removal from the platform.

**1.7 Operational Risks**
Including but not limited to: 
The business license number is in the process of being canceled, or the legal representative status is flagged as abnormal.

**1.8 Unverifiable Business License Number**
Including but not limited to: 
The business license number cannot be verified due to input errors, such as incorrect entries or confusion between similar characters (e.g., "0" and "O"; "1" and "I").

# Operational Experience
**2.1 Screenshots Not Taken Directly from the Account Dashboard**
Including but not limited to: 
Photos taken of the screen with another device, rather than a direct screenshot (from either PC or mobile);
Screenshots that do not meet category-specific requirements;
Screenshots displaying the account's public view instead of the account management dashboard.

**2.2 Missing Account Information**
Including but not limited to: 
Incomplete screenshots;
Screenshots without visible login account details;
Missing or incomplete account information.

**2.3 Non-Compliance with Platform Operational Standards**
Including but not limited to: 
Inappropriate content in LIVE sessions.

# Company Introduction
**3.1 Incomplete Office Environment Display**
Including but not limited to: 
Missing photos of the main entrance or reception area (e.g., no company logo visible);
Mismatched entity information (e.g., the office logo does not match the company name or logo on the application);
Non-original images (e.g., photos with watermarks from other platforms).
> **Requirements**
> **Three photos:**

> * Reception area with company logo
> * General office area
> * Live-streaming or meeting rooms

>  **One video:**

> * A single, continuous, unedited video shot from a straightforward angle, clearly showing the surroundings.
> * The video must include the company logo on the main entrance, office area, live-streaming rooms, and meeting rooms.


**3.2 Office Environment Does Not Match the Company's Described Size**
Including but not limited to:
Office capacity does not align with the company's description (e.g., the number of workspaces or employees does not match the company's introduction).

**3.3 Missing Company Operations Description**
Including but not limited to: 
No description of the company's main business activities.

**3.4 Missing Company Showcases**
Including but not limited to: 
No showcase materials provided, or submitted examples are not related to the company.

**3.5 Logo Inconsistency with Application Company**
Including but not limited to: 
For example, the logo says "ABC Company," but the application is for "XAS Company."

**3.6 Account Used for External Promotion**
Including but not limited to: 
Account names containing email addresses, phone numbers, company addresses, or other contact information.

**3.7 Inconsistent Entity Information**
The company name displayed at the front desk or storefront does not match the information provided in the application.

> * If the storefront name is in Chinese, it must match the company name on the business license.
> * If the storefront name is in English, the information in the "partner_name" field must either overlap with or be identical to the storefront name.
> * If the storefront name is bilingual, the Chinese name should match the company name on the business license, while the English name must overlap with or be identical to the "partner_name" field in the application.


**3.8 Company Size Does Not Meet Onboarding Requirements**
Including but not limited to:
Office capacity or number of live-streaming rooms does not meet onboarding requirements;
Photos are taken outside working hours, showing no staff present.

**3.9 Low-Quality Live-streaming Rooms**
Including but not limited to: 
Live-streaming rooms are poorly equipped or not actively in use.

# Impersonation of Official TikTok Shop or Other TikTok Affiliated Entities
**4.1 Office Decor Featuring TikTok Shop or Affiliated Logos**
Including but not limited to: 
Displaying TikTok Shop or affiliated company logos or text in areas such as the company's reception, signage, or glass door stickers.

**4.2 Materials Featuring TikTok Shop or Affiliated Logos**
Including but not limited to: 
Use of homophonic company names (including account names) or materials that resemble official TikTok logos or text;
Inclusion of TikTok logos or text in company introduction slides or promotional materials.
