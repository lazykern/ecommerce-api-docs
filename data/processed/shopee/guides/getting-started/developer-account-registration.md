#  2\. **Understanding Shopee Open Platform account types**

 

  1. **Individual Seller** -**** Accounts of individuals who sell on Shopee without a business license.(Only support service your Shopee shop.)
  2. **Registered Business Seller** \- Accounts of businesses selling on Shopee with a registered license.(Only support service your Shopee shop.)
  3. **Third-party Partner Platform (ISV)** \- Accounts of businesses with registered licenses providing services to sellers on Shopee.



 

**You can use the Account Type Selection table below to see which account type you should choose. Note that:**

  * The APPs you can create on Shopee Open Platform depends on the account type you apply for. Refer to [_App management page_](<https://open.shopee.com/developer-guide/14>) __ for details.
  * Additionally, you need to meet the Shopee Open Platform Eligibility Criteria based on the markets you’re operating in and the account type you want to apply for.

### **3.2 Third-party Partner Platforms**

 

To gain access to the Shopee Open API Platform, you have to meet the following criteria:

 

  1. You’re a registered business. Provide valid business documents during your application.
  2. Your business product has to be live, with existing ecommerce integrations which are identifiable via a provided trial account.
  3. Your services should not include suspicious activities such as the selling of cross-border accounts, or extracting listing information to enable transactions outside of Shopee. You may refer to our [_Platform Partner Rules_](<https://open.shopee.com/policy?policy_id=2>) for more information.
  4. Submit live versions of (a) your business product’s URL, (b) trial account login details and enable all features for Shopee’s testing purposes.
  5. Your live business product URL must utilize a TLS 1.2 Protocol and begin with “https://”.



 

If you are a developer registered in the regions stated below, take note of the additional criteria that applies to you:  
  
**TW (Taiwan)**

  1. Your existing ecommerce integration(s) must be with a major ecommerce platform that has regulations for user data privacy. 



 

**CB-CN (Cross Border-China)**

  1. The business scope of your business license must be software development.
  2. Your registered capital must be higher than 100,000 RMB.
  3. You should have notified your Account Manager regarding your application.



 

**CB-KR (Cross Border-Korea), CB-JP (Cross Border-Japan)**

  1. You should have notified your Account Manager regarding your application.



# 4.Submitting profile details for approval

## 4.1 Log in to Shopee Open Platform > Console > **App Management** > **App List** >**** choose an account type depending on which description suits your business profile better. 

 

_⚠️Note: See the** _Understanding Shopee Open Platform account types_** section below for more details on eligibility._

* **Shopee Open Platform Console** : Refers to the site for you to manage all Shopee Open Platform matters.

## 4.2 Fill in the details according to your account type

 

**A. Shopee Seller > Individual Seller**

**B. Shopee Seller > Registered Business Seller**

**C. Third-party Partner Platform**

### **4.2.1 Shopee Seller** >**Individual Seller**

 

i) After selecting **Shopee Seller** as your account type, you will be redirected to verify your Shopee seller account. Fill in the correct Shopee seller account credentials > select **Log In** to complete verification.

![](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=%2FZhUnq%2B3isJfdTIkyLgSYbwa0LvY8LZpO%2BRs3kAuU%2Ftv2KBmG3WyRvrfxNr4wQ9CvXzLGiojmck3CmCkt3UPmw%3D%3D&image_type=png)

ii) If your seller account verification is successful, you can continue to fill in other Shopee Seller Identification details. 

Select **Individual Seller** from the **Seller Type** dropdown menu > fill in your **Key Account Manager’s Email** > select **Next**.

⚠️ _Note:_ Key Account Manager's Email should be a valid Shopee email (ends with @shopee.com). If no Key Account Manager's Email, please leave it blank.

 

![](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=0dVZpDHPzWsv%2Fj9fGDtqIMc%2Bg5r2%2FVhCdh5gdllOsrfOoIQl9w6SzkImKogD%2BHU43r8WTyUvFYnPCCQ4NGV%2BDA%3D%3D&image_type=png)

iii) Fill in all profile details > select **Submit**.

![](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=NOaT74t3dNVAGad136U3izXC0NO0Uc0llk8G%2FGOhd92gDLJv4RAluuRgasxfSA%2B701M2RkxdVuiACkhohw4ZnQ%3D%3D&image_type=png)

 

### 4.2.2 **Shopee Seller** > **Registered Business Seller**

 

i) After selecting **Shopee Seller** as your account type, you will be redirected to verify your Shopee seller account Fill in the correct Shopee seller account credentials > select **Log in** to complete verification.

⚠️ _Note: Key Account Manager's Email should be a valid Shopee email (ends with @shopee.com). If no Key Account Manager's Email, please leave it blank._

### **4.2.3 Third-party Partner Platform**

 

i) After selecting **Third-party Partner Platform  ** as your account type, you will be redirected to the **Business Information** section.

Fill in all profile details > select **Next**.

![](https://open.shopee.com/opservice/api/v1/image/download/?image_id=%2BwQ5xfSYqhXcQ9NcXUtbMoDHOyYdSsSI3LuRxT9uudVT%2FquD9SG4LY7aLdhFAwwbuQ1WNwhIWfGNiKVjdhZERg%3D%3D&image_type=png)

ii) Under the **Services Information** section, fill in your software service details and select **Submit**.

⚠️ _Note: Provide your software login address and live testing account for Shopee’s verification. URL should start with “https://” and utilize a TLS 1.2 Protocol._

 

## 4.3 Wait for your profile to be approved by Shopee

Once your application is approved, you will receive a notification email via your submitted email address. 

_⚠️ Note:**Shopee Seller** applications will be processed in 3 working days, while **Th**_**ird-party Partner Platform** applications will be processed in 10 working days.
