If you are new to Lazada Open Platform, this guide would help you step by step how to onboard the platform, start the development of your application with Lazada API, deploy your application online, and share your application with Lazada sellers.

  1. **_[Become a developer](<https://open.lazada.com/apps/doc/doc?nodeId=10396&docId=108001>)_** \- Register a UAC (unified account center) account and sign up as a Lazada developer.
  2. **_[Register your application](<https://open.lazada.com/apps/doc/doc?nodeId=10398&docId=108002>)_** in one of the application categories - Submit the request and Lazada admins will review and approve your request.
  3. **_[Retrieve App Key and App Secret](<https://open.lazada.com/apps/doc/doc?nodeId=10433&docId=108055>)_** of your application - The unique identity of your application on the Lazada Open Platform.
  4. **_[Request API permission](<https://open.lazada.com/apps/doc/doc?nodeId=10535&docId=108131>)_** for your application, so that your application can initiate calls to Lazada APIs.
  5. **_[Start development of your application](<https://open.lazada.com/apps/doc/doc?nodeId=10536&docId=108132>)_** \- Learn how to interact with Lazada API. You could find more technical details from the Developer’s Guide section.
